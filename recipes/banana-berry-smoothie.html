Banana Berry Smoothie

Smoothie made from bananas and frozen berry blend

Ingredients

  • One whole banana
  • 3/4 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 scoop whey protien powder
  • 6 large ice cubes
  • 1.5 cups milk

Steps

  1. Add ingredient items to blender in order above
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Pour into face hole
