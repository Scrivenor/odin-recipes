Country Sweet BBQ Chicken Pizza

Dough Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons actuve ddry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon cornmeal

Topping Ingredients

  • 1 grilled chicken breast (cut into bite size pieces)
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1.5 tablespoon Kerrygold butter
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon coarse seasalt
  • 1/3 cup Country Sweet BBQ Sauce

Dough Steps

  1. Add water, flour, salt, and yeast to breadmaker pan
  2. Set breadmaker to dough setting (1.5 hours)
  3. Remove dough from bread pan.
  4. form dough into pizza shape. (add flour as necessary to prvent sticking)
  5. Sprinkle cornmeal on to pizza peel evenly
  6. Turn dough out on to pizza peel and verify the dough does not stick.

Topping Steps

  1. Melt butter, brush butter on edge of dough
  2. Sprinkle garlic poweder and seasalt on edge of dough over butter
  3. Spread BBQ Sauce evenly on dough
  4. Spread chicken pieces and red onion evenly on pizza
  5. Spread cheese over toppings

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat overn to 550
  2. Slide pizza on to heated pizza stone
  3. Bake for approximately 10 minutes or until cheese has started to brown
  4. Stuff in to face hole
